Itaewon Class

Park Sae-ro-yi's life is turned upside down after he is expelled from school and his father is killed in an accident caused by the heir of the powerful Jangga Group. After serving time in prison, Sae-ro-yi moves to Itaewon with the goal of opening his own bar, DanBam, and defeating the ruthless Jangga Group. He is joined by Jo Yi-seo, a highly intelligent but sociopathic manager, and a diverse group of employees. This powerful story of revenge, ambition, and entrepreneurship follows Sae-ro-yi's relentless pursuit of success and justice in the vibrant and diverse neighborhood of Itaewon.
