Signal

Park Hae-young, a criminal profiler in the year 2015, discovers a mysterious, antiquated walkie-talkie that allows him to communicate with detective Lee Jae-han, who is living in the year 1989. The walkie-talkie only works at specific times, but the two men use this connection to solve cold cases from the past, including several famous unsolved crimes in Korea. However, changing the past brings dangerous and unintended consequences to the present, putting Hae-young's and his senior detective Cha Soo-hyun's lives at risk as they delve deeper into the corruption surrounding Jae-han's disappearance.
