You Are My Lover Friend (舍不得星星)

Tang Yang and Jiang Shiyan have been inseparable friends since high school. Ten years later, Tang Yang works in bank credit review, helping military families, while Jiang Shiyan leads a successful food media company. Inspired by her clients' stories, Shiyan creates a documentary that brings them closer, eventually igniting long-hidden romantic feelings. As they transition from best friends to life partners, their unwavering trust and commitment are tested by business conspiracies and workplace challenges.
