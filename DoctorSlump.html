Doctor Slump (닥터슬럼프)

Yeo Jeong-woo (Park Hyung-sik) and Nam Ha-neul (Park Shin-hye) were intense academic rivals in high school who both grew up to become successful doctors. However, their lives take a turn when Jeong-woo is accused of medical negligence and Ha-neul resigns from her job due to clinical depression and workplace abuse. Forced to share a rooftop studio, the former rivals become solace for one another as they navigate their respective slumps and rediscover love and personal healing.
