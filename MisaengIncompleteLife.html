Misaeng: Incomplete Life

Jang Geu-rae, whose life goal was to become a professional baduk (Go) player, fails to achieve his dream. Through an acquaintance's recommendation, he lands an internship at a major trading company, One International. Despite having no college education or formal work experience, he struggles to adapt to the highly competitive corporate world. With the help of his supportive boss, Oh Sang-shik, and his challenging but ultimately kind colleagues, Geu-rae slowly learns the rules of survival in the jungle-like office environment, finding his own path to completing his "incomplete life."
