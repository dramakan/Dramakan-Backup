Only for Love (以爱为营)

Zheng Shu Yi (Bai Lu) is a high-achieving financial reporter for a major newspaper who is determined to land an exclusive interview with the enigmatic CEO Shi Yan (Dylan Wang). After a misunderstanding brings them together, Shu Yi sets aside her embarrassment to win him over for the interview, while Shi Yan is intrigued by her deep knowledge of startups. As they collaborate professionally, their rivalry turns into a complex romance filled with flirty moments and corporate intrigue. The story is adapted from the novel "Accidental Love" (Cuo Liao) by Qiao Yao.
