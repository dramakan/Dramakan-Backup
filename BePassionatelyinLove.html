Be Passionately in Love (陷入我们的热恋)

The story begins in the rainy southern city of Qingyi. Xu Zhi, the "Ruijun Dark Horse" and top college entrance exam scorer, meets Chen Lu Zhou, the "Naked Score Topper," which begins their intense romance despite an initial misunderstanding. Their budding relationship is paused when Lu Zhou is sent abroad by his family and Xu Zhi moves to Beijing to study architecture. However, Lu Zhou eventually gives up his overseas studies, returns to China, and enrolls in the same university as Xu Zhi to passionately pursue her. The drama is an adaptation of the web novel by Er Dong Tu Zi.
