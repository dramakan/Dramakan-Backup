Suspicious Partner

Noh Ji-wook is a highly successful prosecutor who is forced to change careers and become a private attorney. Eun Bong-hee is a bright, energetic intern who becomes the prime suspect in a sudden murder case. Though initially antagonists, Ji-wook defends Bong-hee. After she is cleared, they end up working together at his law firm. As they tackle mysterious cases, they realize the murder Bong-hee was accused of is connected to a larger serial killer case, and that both of their traumatic pasts are intertwined. Their professional partnership blossoms into a complex, suspenseful, and often hilarious romance.
