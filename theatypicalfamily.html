The Atypical Family ( )

The Bok family possesses supernatural powers, but each member has lost their abilities due to modern-day struggles. **Bok Gwi-ju** (Jang Ki-yong) can revisit happy memories by traveling back in time but lost his power due to depression after his wife's death. His mother, **Bok Man-heum** (Go Doo-shim), lost her precognitive dreaming ability due to insomnia, and his sister, **Bok Dong-hee** (Claudia Kim), lost her ability to fly due to struggles with obesity. Their lives change when they become intertwined with **Do Da-hae** (Chun Woo-hee), a mysterious con artist, who inadvertently helps Gwi-ju shrug off depression and begins to regain his ability.
