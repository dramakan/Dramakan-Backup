Summit of Our Youth (我在顶峰等你)

Gu Xuejiao (Sun Zhenni), an office worker struggling in her career and burdened by past regrets, unexpectedly travels back to her senior year of high school after an elevator crash. Determined to change her fate, she transforms from a romance-addicted slacker into an ambitious scholar, seeking guidance from the brilliant top student Lin Zhihua (Chen Jingke). As they navigate high school again, they inspire each other to reach new heights and rediscover a sweet, mutual love they never expected in their original timeline. The drama is adapted from the novel "Tan Lian Ai Bu Ru Shang Qing Hua".
