Alchemy of Souls ()

Set in the fictional country of Daeho, the story follows young mages whose destinies are altered due to the forbidden magic known as the "alchemy of souls," which allows souls to shift bodies. Jang Uk, a young nobleman whose gate of energy was blocked, finds a master in Naksu, a skilled assassin whose soul has shifted into the body of the servant girl Mu-deok. As they train and navigate the political intrigue of Daeho, they uncover secrets and an epic love story unfolds. The series is split into two parts: Part 1 (20 episodes) and Part 2, titled *Alchemy of Souls: Light and Shadow* (10 episodes), for a total of 30 episodes.
