Our Generation (樱桃琥珀)

Lin Qile (Zhao Jinmai), a spirited girl growing up in a close-knit community, forms a deep bond with Jiang Jiaoxi (Zhang Linghe), a mysterious and talented "math genius" who briefly moves to her town. After years of separation, they reconnect in high school and eventually navigate their way through adulthood together. Spanning three decades, the story explores themes of courage, independence, and the enduring power of family and first love. The drama is adapted from the novel "Cherry Amber" by Yun Zhu.
