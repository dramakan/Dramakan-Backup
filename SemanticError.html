Semantic Error

Chu Sang-woo is a rigid, rule-abiding computer science major who excels at coding but is socially awkward. To avoid group projects, he removes the names of absent classmates, including one named Jang Jae-young, from his liberal arts presentation. Jae-young, a popular, flamboyant visual design major, is furious that this "error" cost him his graduation credit. He decides to ruthlessly annoy Sang-woo, setting out to make his life miserable. However, their constant clashes evolve into a complex, fiery, and unexpected romantic attraction, where the methodical engineer and the expressive designer clash and complement each other.
