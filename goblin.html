Guardian: The Lonely and Great God (Goblin)

Kim Shin (Gong Yoo), a decorated military general in the Goryeo Dynasty, is betrayed and killed by the young king. He is cursed by God to live forever as an immortal **Goblin**, with a sword perpetually stuck in his chest, enduring the pain of watching his loved ones die. He seeks a prophesied **human bride** (Ji Eun-Tak, played by Kim Go-Eun) who can draw the sword and grant him release. He becomes accidental roommates with an amnesiac **Grim Reaper** (Lee Dong-Wook). The story follows their interconnected fates and the complicated romance between the Goblin and his bride, Ji Eun-Tak.
