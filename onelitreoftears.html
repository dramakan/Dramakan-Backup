One Liter of Tears (Ichi Rittoru no Namida)

Based on a true story, the drama follows Aya Ikeuchi, a bright and energetic 15-year-old girl who is diagnosed with Spinocerebellar Ataxia (SCA), a rare, incurable disease that slowly affects the cerebellum, leading to a loss of balance, coordination, and control over her body, while leaving her mind intact. The story chronicles her struggle to live her life to the fullest, her attempts to adapt to her deteriorating condition, and her family's unwavering support, all documented through her heartfelt diary entries.
