A Girl and Three Sweethearts

Misaki Sakurai is a pastry chef who has dedicated her life to her work, neglecting romance. Suddenly unemployed, she runs into her first love from high school, Chiaki Shibasaki. Chiaki offers Misaki a job and a place to stay at his family's seaside restaurant in Shonan. She moves in, expecting a dream job, only to find herself living with Chiaki and his two younger, equally handsome brothers: Kanata, the cold head chef who constantly criticizes her desserts, and Toma, the friendly youngest brother. Misaki finds herself navigating complicated feelings as she works and lives under one roof with the three distinct siblings.
