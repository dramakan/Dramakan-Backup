Extraordinary You

Eun Dan-oh is a high school student from a wealthy family who discovers she is just a supporting character inside a Korean manhwa (comic). She also realizes that her actions are predetermined by the Artist. Determined to find her own free will and true love, she decides to go against the story's script and changes her destiny. She meets 'Number 13,' a nameless character who seems to be the only one capable of altering the plot with her. The drama follows their extraordinary journey to write their own story outside the bounds of the comic's 'Stage.'
