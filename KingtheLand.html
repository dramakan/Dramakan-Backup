Gu Won, the handsome and intelligent heir of The King Group, is thrown into a fierce inheritance war. He's a cynical man who despises fake smiles, constantly searching for the truth about his mother. Sa-rang finds herself working in "King the Land," a lounge for VIP guests, where she continuously runs into Gu Won.
