Her Private Life

Sung Deok-mi is a talented chief curator at an art gallery. She is also secretly a dedicated fangirl of idol Cha Shi-an and runs a popular fansite dedicated to him. Her private life is thrown into chaos when Ryan Gold, a former famous painter, becomes the new director of the gallery. Ryan discovers Deok-mi's secret double life and their professional relationship quickly evolves as they become entangled in a fake dating relationship to protect her secret. The drama follows their journey from co-workers to fake lovers to real romance, all while navigating the world of art and passionate K-Pop fandom.
