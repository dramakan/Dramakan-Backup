My Dearest Nemesis ( )

This series is based on the webtoon *He's a Black Dragon*. It tells the story of **Baek Soo-jung** (Moon Ga-young), a hardworking planning team leader at Yongseong Department Store, who encounters her past when **Ban Joo-yeon** (Choi Hyun-wook), the new strategic planning director and company heir, becomes her boss. Sixteen years earlier, they met in an online game where Joo-yeon, then a middle schooler using the nickname "Black Dragon," confessed his feelings to Soo-jung and was swiftly rejected, leading to humiliation and disaster. Now, fate forces them to confront past regrets and shifting power dynamics in the real world.
