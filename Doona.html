Doona! (이두나!)

Lee Doo-na (Bae Suzy), a star member of the K-pop group Dream Sweet, suddenly announces her retirement and retreats to a university share house. There, she meets Lee Won-jun (Yang Se-jong), a warm-hearted and ordinary student who initially has no idea who she is. As Won-jun's kindness provides Doo-na comfort from her past trauma, the two navigate an unlikely romance while dealing with the pressures of fame and adulthood. The drama is adapted from the webtoon "The Girl Downstairs".
