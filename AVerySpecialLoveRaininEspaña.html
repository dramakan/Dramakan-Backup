A Very Special Love (Rain in España)

The series follows the story of Luna, an architecture student, and Kalix, a legal management student, who meet and fall in love while studying in the university district of España. Their relationship navigates the complexities of young love, career aspirations, and personal growth. Luna dreams of becoming a successful architect, while Kalix is set on becoming a lawyer. However, the ambitious paths they choose lead them down different routes, testing their bond and forcing them to confront difficult choices about their future together. The series is based on the popular Wattpad novel of the same name by Gwy Saludes, and is the first installment of the "A Very Special Love" trilogy.
