Ideal Boyfriend (理想ノカレシ)

Yumeko Onodera (Aya Omasa) is a successful professional who is secretly messy at home and clumsy in romance. After a painful breakup and workplace betrayal, she encounters Hikaru Murase (Taiki Sato), a mysterious young man who is the spitting image of her high school first love. Heartbroken and intrigued, Yumeko proposes a "contract relationship" where she can raise him into her "ideal boyfriend". As their unusual arrangement develops, she must navigate complex emotions between her memories of the past and the reality of the present.
