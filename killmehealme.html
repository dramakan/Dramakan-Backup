Cha Do-hyun is a third-generation chaebol suffering from Dissociative Identity Disorder (DID), brought on by a traumatic past event, leading to seven distinct personalities. He struggles to keep his condition a secret until he meets Oh Ri-jin, a first-year psychiatric resident who secretly agrees to treat him. As she helps him manage his various alters—including the rebellious Shin Se-gi and the suicidal Ahn Yo-sub—they slowly uncover a deeply buried secret that ties their pasts together, leading to a path of healing and unexpected love.
