Hong Ji-ah is a cold, tough-as-nails exorcist who runs Daebak Real Estate, a company specializing in selling properties haunted by lingering spirits. She meets Oh In-beom, a clever con artist who pretends to be an exorcist to exploit people, but he actually possesses psychic abilities that can sense and channel spirits. Ji-ah and In-beom reluctantly form a partnership: she performs the exorcisms, and he acts as the medium, working together to clear properties and uncover a painful secret tied to both their pasts.
