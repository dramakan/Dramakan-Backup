Legend of the Blue Sea

Shim Cheong is the last mermaid on Earth. She encounters Heo Joon-jae, a genius con artist, after traveling across the ocean to modern-day Seoul. The drama alternates between their present-day romance and their past incarnations in the Joseon era, where Shim Cheong was Se-hwa and Joon-jae was a kind district magistrate named Dam-ryeong. The story follows Shim Cheong's hilarious attempts to adapt to human life and Joon-jae's growing realization that their fates are deeply and tragically intertwined across centuries.
