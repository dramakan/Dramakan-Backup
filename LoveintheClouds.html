Love in the Clouds (入青云)

At the annual Qingyun Conference, warrior Ji Bozai (Hou Minghao) defeats the reigning warrior goddess Ming Yi (Lu Yuxiao). After her spiritual vein is broken and she is poisoned, Ming Yi disguises herself as a dancer to get close to Ji Bozai to find the antidote. Under their carefully crafted personas, the two engage in a thrilling dance of deception and attraction while fighting for justice in the Six Realms. The drama is adapted from the web novel by Bai Lu Cheng Shuang.
