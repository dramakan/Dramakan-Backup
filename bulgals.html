Bulgasal: Immortal Souls ()

The series tells the story of a man, **Dan Hwal** (Lee Jin-wook), who was human but became a **tt14691616** (a mythical creature cursed with immortality) 600 years ago, and cannot kill nor be killed. He chases a woman, **Min Sang-woon** (Kwon Nara), who has experienced death and **reincarnation** for 600 years, retaining memories of her past lives. Hwal is driven by vengeance against the woman who turned him into the creature, while Sang-woon holds the key to his salvation. Their story is a tragic love saga spanning ancient and modern times.
