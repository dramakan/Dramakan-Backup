My Love Mix-Up!

Aoki, a kind-hearted high school student, has a crush on Hashimoto, the girl who sits next to him. When he borrows her eraser, he's crushed to see she's written the name of another boy, Ida, on it. To make matters worse, Ida sees Aoki holding the eraser and mistakenly believes Aoki has a crush on him! A simple, innocent high school crush snowballs into a hilarious and heartwarming comedy of errors.
