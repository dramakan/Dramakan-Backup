Reply 1988 ( 1988)

Set in the year **1988** during the Seoul Olympics, this nostalgic drama revolves around **five childhood friends** (Deok-sun, Jung-hwan, Taek, Sun-woo, and Dong-ryong) and their families living in the close-knit neighborhood of Ssangmun-dong. The series uses the classic *Reply* format of moving between past and present, making viewers guess which of Deok-sun's male friends she eventually marries. The show is a cultural phenomenon celebrated for its deep focus on **family dynamics**, community bonds, and the nostalgia of the era.
