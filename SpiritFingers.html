Spirit Fingers (스피릿 핑거스)

Song Woo Yeon (Park Ji Hu) is a shy high school student who feels utterly unremarkable in a family of overachievers. Her life changes when she is invited to join "Spirit Fingers," an eccentric croquis art club that values passion and self-expression over technical skill. Surrounded by vibrant club members, she begins to discover her own voice, identity, and a budding romance with the charismatic Nam Gi Jeong (Jo Joon Young). The drama is adapted from the popular Naver webtoon by Han Kyoung Chal.
