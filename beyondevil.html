Beyond Evil

Lee Dong-sik, a former promising police officer now working at a local police station in the quiet town of Manyang, and Han Joo-won, a highly-skilled elite detective from the metropolitan police, are two men whose lives become intertwined after a murder case resurfaces in their quiet town of Manyang. Dong-sik was once a promising police officer, but now works at a local police station. Joo-won, a highly-skilled elite detective, is transferred to the same station and becomes Dong-sik's superior. The two must work together to find the killer, constantly questioning each other's innocence and motives, pushing the limits of their alliance and sanity as they pursue the true "monster" behind the killings.
