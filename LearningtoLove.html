Learning to Love (愛の学校)

Ogawa Manami (Fumino Kimura) is a serious and Passionate high school teacher raised in a strict household who feels suffocated by societal expectations. Her world changes when she meets Kaoru (Raul), a popular host in Kabukicho who secretly struggles with illiteracy. What starts as a secret tutoring arrangement to help Kaoru read and write develops into a deep, forbidden bond that challenges class divisions and their own self-worth. As they navigate a severe world filled with judgment and jealousy, they must decide if their connection is worth the controversy.
