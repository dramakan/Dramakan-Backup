Good Doctor (J-Drama)

Minato Shindo is a young man who has Savant Syndrome, which gives him extraordinary memory and spatial skills, but he struggles greatly with social interaction. His lifelong dream is to become a pediatrician. Against internal hospital opposition, a doctor helps Minato join a prestigious university hospital as a resident. Despite his challenges, Minato's sincerity, unique perspective, and remarkable ability to memorize vast amounts of medical knowledge allow him to approach pediatric patients in a way his colleagues cannot. The series follows his journey to prove his value and save children's lives while fighting prejudice and adapting to a high-pressure environment.
