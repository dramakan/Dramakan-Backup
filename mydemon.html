My Demon ( )

The series depicts the story of a fateful encounter between Do Do-hee (Kim Yoo-jung), a haughty but organized heiress of a conglomerate, and Jeong Gu-won (Song Kang), a demon who is cold and carefree. Gu-won is a 200-year-old demon who makes tempting soul-binding contracts with humans. However, he accidentally loses his powers after his demonic tattoo is transferred to Do-hee. In a desperate bid for survival and to reclaim his powers, he enters into a contract marriage with Do-hee, becoming her pseudo bodyguard and setting the stage for their enemies-to-lovers story.
