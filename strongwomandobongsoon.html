Strong Woman Do Bong Soon

Do Bong-soon is a young woman born with superhuman strength, a gift passed down only to the women in her family. Her dream is to create a video game featuring herself as the main character. Due to her strength, she is hired by Ahn Min-hyuk, the eccentric and playful CEO of a gaming company (AinSoft), as his personal bodyguard after he receives anonymous threats. A love triangle forms between Bong-soon, Min-hyuk, and her childhood crush, police officer In Kook-doo, while they investigate a series of abductions in her neighborhood.
