From Five to Nine

Junko Sakuraba is a 29-year-old ambitious English conversation teacher who dreams of working in New York. However, she gets tangled in an arranged marriage meeting (omiai) organized by her family. To her horror, her potential partner is Takane Hoshikawa, a handsome but serious and single-minded Buddhist monk. Despite her initial attempts to reject him, Takane is instantly smitten and relentless in his pursuit of Junko, creating chaos in her life. The romantic comedy follows Junko as she navigates her complicated job, multiple love interests, and the monk who refuses to take 'no' for an answer, forcing her to rethink her dreams and her rigid expectations for life and love.
