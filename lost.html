Lost

Titled *Human Disqualification* (, Ingansilgyeok) in Korean, the story follows two lonely individuals grappling with a sense of failure and emptiness. Boo-jung is a ghostwriter in her 40s who feels lost and directionless, convinced she has achieved nothing in life. Kang-jae is a 27-year-old man who runs a service where he acts as an escort or companion for others, finding himself perpetually disconnected from life. The drama explores the quiet, poignant journey of these two people meeting at the lowest points of their lives and seeking comfort and meaning in their shared state of being 'lost.'
