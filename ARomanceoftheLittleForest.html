A Romance of the Little Forest (两个人的小森林)

Yu Mei Ren (Esther Yu) is a successful fashion blogger who relies on makeup to maintain her stunning appearance. Years ago, she was unfeelingly rejected by Zhuang Yu (Vin Zhang), a brilliant botanist devoted entirely to his research. When their paths cross again, Mei Ren decides to use her beauty to make him fall for her as a form of "revenge". However, as she navigates their encounter from the bustling city to the scenic countryside, she discovers his hidden depth and finds herself falling for him all over again. The story is a sweet "enemies-to-lovers" tale of self-confidence and second chances.
