Lovestruck in the City

Told in a unique pseudo-documentary format, this drama follows the romantic lives of six young adults living in a complex and fast-paced city. The central story revolves around Park Jae-won, a passionate architect, and Lee Eun-oh, a freelance marketer who adopted a free-spirited alter ego, "Yoon Sun-a," during a summer romance. They fell deeply in love, only for Eun-oh/Sun-a to disappear without a trace. Through candid interviews, the characters explore their differing views on dating, heartbreak, and finding romance in the bustling metropolitan landscape, leading Jae-won to desperately search for the woman who stole his camera and heart.
