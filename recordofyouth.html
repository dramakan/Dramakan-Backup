Record of Youth

The drama follows the lives of three ambitious young individuals in the highly competitive fashion industry in Itaewon, Seoul. Sa Hye-joon is a passionate model who strives to become an actor but constantly struggles with failed auditions and part-time jobs. His best friend, Won Hae-hyo, is also a model but comes from a wealthy family, giving him an easier path. Ahn Jeong-ha is a talented makeup artist who leaves her corporate job to pursue her passion. The series focuses on their dreams, struggles with love and friendship, and their individual efforts to make a name for themselves in a world that values background over talent.
