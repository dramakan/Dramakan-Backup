Bong Sang-pil is a former gangster who witnessed his mother's murder as a child. He becomes a lawyer to avenge her death, often bending the rules and using his fists along with the law. He returns to his hometown of Gisung to open a 'Lawless Law Firm' and fight the corrupt officials and powers that run the city, chief among them a ruthless judge, Cha Moon-sook, and a former gangster turned CEO, An Oh-joo. He partners with Ha Jae-yi, a principled lawyer suspended for assaulting a judge, and together they seek justice in a lawless city.
