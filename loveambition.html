Love's Ambition (许我耀眼)

Xu Yan (Zhao Lusi), a popular TV host from a small town, has built a polished public persona to achieve success in the big city. She marries elite businessman Shen Haoming (William Chan), but their marriage is tested by their different backgrounds and Haoming's controlling nature. When Xu Yan decides to break free and rebuild herself from scratch, Haoming realizes he has truly fallen for her and determinedly tries to win her back, leading them both on a journey of self-discovery and true vulnerability. The drama is adapted from the novel "Da Qiao Xiao Qiao" by Zhang Yue Ran.
