Love on the Turquoise Land (枭起青壤)

Nie Jiu Luo (Dilraba Dilmurat), a descendant of a secret lineage of "Earth-Knockers," leads a hidden life as a modern sculptor while guarding ancient secrets. She meets Yan Tuo (Chen Xing Xu), a wealthy young man whose family is entangled in a deep supernatural conspiracy. As they venture into the mysterious turquoise lands, they uncover the truth about the "Earth-Knocker" legend and face dark forces that threaten both their worlds. The series is adapted from the novel "Xiao Qi Qing Rang" by Wei Yu.
