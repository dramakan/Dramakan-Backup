This series, based on a popular webtoon, tells the story of **Yoon Ga-min** (Hwang Min-hyun), a student at the notorious Yusung Technical High School. Despite studying hard, Ga-min's grades don't improve, but he is **"only good at fighting"**. He forms a study group at the school with the goal of entering college. When his study group friends are bullied by the violent students who control the school, Ga-min must take off his glasses and uniform jacket to put his fighting skills to use to defend his friends and his goal.
