Healer

Seo Jung-hoo is the "Healer," a night courier with exceptional fighting skills who performs any task for a high price, except murder. His life changes when he takes on a job from a famous reporter, Kim Moon-ho, to find a woman named Chae Young-shin, an enthusiastic but clumsy online news reporter. As Jung-hoo gets closer to Young-shin under a false identity, they uncover a deeply buried secret involving their parents and a tragic incident from 1992, forcing them to fight against powerful enemies seeking to conceal the truth.
