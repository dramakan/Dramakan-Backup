Ha Eun-gyeol is a CODA (Child of Deaf Adults) and the only person who can hear in his family. He leads a double life as a talented student by day and a passionate guitarist by night. One day, he accidentally time travels through a strange music shop back to the year 1995. There, he meets his high school aged father, Lee Chan, and is shocked to discover his father can hear. Eun-gyeol forms a band with Lee Chan and other mysterious friends. He attempts to interfere with his parents' future, seeking to prevent an accident that will eventually leave his father hearing-impaired, but finds that altering the past is more complicated—and dangerous—than he imagined.
