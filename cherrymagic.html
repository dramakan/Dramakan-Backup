Cherry Magic!

Kiyoshi Adachi, a shy office worker, reaches his 30th birthday as a virgin and gains a magical power: the ability to read the minds of anyone he touches. Overwhelmed by his new ability, he tries to avoid people, but one day accidentally touches the most handsome and popular salesman in the office, Yuichi Kurosawa. To his shock, Adachi discovers that his perfect colleague is deeply in love with him.
