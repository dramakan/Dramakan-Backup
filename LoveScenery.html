Love Scenery (良辰美景好时光)

The drama follows the seemingly distant relationship between Liang Chen, a charismatic singer known for her beautiful stage presence, and Lu Jing, a gifted university student specializing in computer science. Despite their differences, their paths cross due to their love for gaming. Lu Jing, who is secretly a big fan of Liang Chen's music, ends up becoming her gaming partner, initially unaware that the "noob" he is coaching online is his idol. Their virtual collaboration leads to a sweet romance as they navigate the challenges of their respective careers. The drama is based on the novel "Ming Qi Qian Li Ke" by Qiao Yao.
