Doom at Your Service

Tak Dong-kyung is an editor who is given a terminal diagnosis and has only 100 days left to live. In her despair, she wishes for the world to be ruined. Her wish is heard by Myul Mang, a messenger between gods and humans who is literally 'Doom'. He offers her a contract: she can live her remaining days as she wishes, but at the end, she must wish for the doom of the world, or the person she loves most will die instead. As they spend time together, a complicated romance blossoms.
