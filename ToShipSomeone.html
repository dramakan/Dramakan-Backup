To Ship Someone (全世界都在等你们分手)

Ji Shu (Zhu Zheng Ting) is a pessimistic novelist famous for his heartbreaking tragic endings, while Song Yan Qi (Lu Yu Xiao) is an optimistic drama producer who lives for happy endings. When Yan Qi attempts to adapt Ji Shu's latest novel with a cheerful twist, the two find themselves sucked into the pages of the story. Forced to live as characters within the novel, they must navigate the plot and rewrite their destinies while discovering that the line between fiction and reality—and between hate and love—is thinner than they thought.
