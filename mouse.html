Jung Ba-reum is an honest and kind rookie police officer who works at a substation. He crosses paths with Go Moo-chi, a brutal detective who is solely focused on revenge against a serial killer who murdered his parents when he was young. Their lives become intertwined as they chase a serial killer whose murderous crimes seem driven by a "psychopathic gene" detectable in utero. The drama asks the fundamental question: if we could identify psychopaths before they commit crimes, would we kill them? This leads into a shocking series of events and intense identity twists.
