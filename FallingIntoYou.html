Falling Into You (炽道)

Duan Yucheng is a high-spirited young man with a passion for high jump but is limited by his height. He meets Luo Na, an experienced athletics assistant who recognizes his potential. Determined to receive her guidance, he enrolls at her university to join her team. Together, they navigate the sweets and cruelties of pursuing professional sports dreams, step by step, as their bond blossoms into love on the blazing track. The drama is based on Twentine's novel.
