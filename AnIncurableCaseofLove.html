An Incurable Case of Love (恋はつづくよどこまでも)

Nanase Sakura (Mone Kamishiraishi) fell in love with Dr. Kairi Tendo (Takeru Satoh) after witnessing him save a life five years ago. Driven by her crush, she studies hard and becomes a rookie nurse at the same hospital, only to find that the charming doctor is actually a cold perfectionist nicknamed the "Demon King". Despite his harsh remarks, Nanase's relentless pursuit of his recognition earns her the nickname "Warrior Chick". As they work together, Tendo's icy heart begins to melt for the tenacious and caring nurse. The series is adapted from the manga by Maki Enjoji.
