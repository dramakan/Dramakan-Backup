Deep Affection Eyes (深情眼)

Ye Meng (Zhang Yuxi), in her thirties, returns to her hometown seeking a quieter life after professional setbacks. There, she encounters Li Jinyu (Bi Wenjun), a mysterious and aloof man whose deeply expressive eyes intrigue her. As she enthusiastically helps him navigate his difficult life, Jinyu gradually opens his heart. Together, they warm and heal each other's hidden wounds in a mature, small-town romance. The drama is adapted from the novel of the same name by Er Dong Tu Zi.
