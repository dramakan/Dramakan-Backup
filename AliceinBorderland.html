Alice in Borderland (今際の国のアリス)

Ryōhei Arisu (Kento Yamazaki), a listless video-game obsessed young man, finds himself in a deserted Tokyo with his two best friends. They are forced to compete in dangerous, sadistic games representing card suits: Spades for physical, Clubs for team, Diamonds for wits, and Hearts for betrayal. As they fight to extend their "visas" and survive, Arisu teams up with mountain climber Usagi (Tao Tsuchiya) to unravel the mystery of the Borderland and find a way back to their world. Adapted from the manga by Haro Aso and directed by Shinsuke Sato.
