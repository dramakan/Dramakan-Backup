9 Years of You (แต่ละปีที่มีเธอ)

Pharan (Aimm Sanpetch), a reserved introvert, and Nabdao (Cherprang Areekul), a vibrant "positive energy" girl, have been close friends since childhood. The series follows their journey from 2016 to 2024, navigating life's ups and downs while always remaining each other's support system. As the years pass, their friendship faces critical turning points that challenge their deep bond and evolving feelings. It is a poignant coming-of-age story about love, heartbreak, and the "one true love" trope portrayed with a realistic touch.
