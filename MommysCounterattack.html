Mommy's Counterattack (妈咪的反攻)

Five years ago, Yun Qingqing (Li Boyang) was framed by her sister and kicked out of the house by her husband, Jiang Ling (Zhao Yibo), while pregnant. After surviving a tragic accident, she returns from abroad as a world-renowned top model and high-profile fashion designer. Now, she is determined to reclaim her son and seek revenge on those who wronged her, only to find that Jiang Ling has been hiding a deep secret about their past. As they collide in the business world, old sparks fly and the truth behind the five-year-old conspiracy begins to surface.
