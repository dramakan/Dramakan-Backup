Seal My Lips with a Kiss (キスでふさいで、バレないで。)

Kaede Sato (Ayaka Konno), a new employee in the sales department, finds herself working late under pressure from her boss Satoru Mizoguchi (Noeru Kawashima). After getting accidentally locked out of the office, she is rescued by her senior coworker and secret crush, Daisuke Shioya (Ryusei Fujii). When Shioya's roommate—who turns out to be Kaede's superior, Mizoguchi—returns home early, Kaede and Shioya are forced to hide in a closet, sparking a tense and intimate love triangle. The drama is adapted from the popular manga by Fudono Fudou.
