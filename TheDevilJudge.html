The Devil Judge

Set in a dystopian version of South Korea where chaos and corruption reign, Judge Kang Yo-han is given the power to preside over a live televised court trial, transforming the courtroom into a reality show where the entire nation participates in sentencing the accused. Yo-han is a dark, enigmatic figure who punishes the corrupt and wealthy without mercy, gaining the nickname 'Devil Judge.' His unconventional and often brutal methods are both celebrated and feared. Rookie Judge Kim Ga-on is assigned to Yo-han's court and begins to suspect Yo-han's true intentions, leading him on a mission to uncover the judge's secrets and determine whether he is a true hero or just another tyrant.
